Ritu Charya

Ayurvedic Seasonal Lifestyle Practices

Ritu Charya is an ancient Ayurvedic practice mentioned in Charaka Samhita (Chapter 6) that guides us to live in harmony with nature's seasonal cycles. By following specific lifestyle and dietary guidelines for each season (Ritu), we can maintain optimal health and prevent diseases throughout the year.
